About RevampLEX

Built to make renovation projects easier, clearer, and more connected.

RevampLEX connects homeowners and contractors through a map-based platform with private project rooms, estimates, bids, documents, video, scheduling, and approvals in one place.

Start as Homeowner Start as Contractor

The renovation process should not feel scattered.

Homeowners often struggle to find the right contractor, compare bids, keep track of photos, review estimates, approve work, and manage communication across texts, emails, phone calls, and paper proposals.

Contractors also need a better way to find serious local projects, create estimates, submit proposals, schedule appointments, and keep project conversations organized.

RevampLEX was created to bring both sides into one clean, private workspace designed around real construction workflow.

Location Find projects and contractors on a map.
Estimation Build cleaner bids, proposals, and invoices.
eXecution Manage communication, documents, signatures, and scheduling.

What RevampLEX is designed to do

The goal is simple: help homeowners choose with confidence and help contractors manage better project opportunities.

For Homeowners

Post renovation projects, receive private bids, compare contractors, share photos and documents, approve proposals, and keep everything organized in one room.

For Contractors

Browse local project opportunities, submit private estimates, use proposal tools, communicate with customers, and manage active jobs without scattered paperwork.

For Projects

Every project can have its own private room with chat, video, photos, PDFs, invoices, appointments, signatures, and project history.

Our approach

RevampLEX is being built around practical jobsite needs, not just another directory listing.

Private bidding Bids are designed to stay private between the homeowner and the submitting contractor.
One project room Photos, documents, estimates, invoices, chat, video, schedule, and approvals live in one workspace.
Better contractor tools Estimating, proposal generation, calculators, and customer communication are built into the contractor workflow.
Map-based discovery Homeowners and contractors can browse opportunities visually by location, trade, and project type.
Clearer decisions Homeowners can compare bids, review scopes, and approve work from the same place.
Built for growth RevampLEX is designed to grow into a full renovation marketplace and project management system.
