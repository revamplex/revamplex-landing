Contractor Terms

Effective Date: May 2026 • These terms apply specifically to contractors, tradespeople, service professionals, companies, and business users using RevampLEX.

Built for serious professionals RevampLEX is designed to connect contractors with real homeowner opportunities through private bidding, project rooms, estimates, scheduling, proposals, and project collaboration tools.

1. Contractor Eligibility

By creating a contractor account, you represent that you are legally authorized to operate your business, provide services, enter contracts, and comply with all applicable licensing, registration, insurance, tax, and local business requirements.

2. Accurate Business Information

Contractors agree to provide accurate and truthful business information, including where applicable:

  • Business name
  • Trade category
  • Service areas
  • Licensing information
  • Insurance information
  • Portfolio images and project examples
  • Years of experience and qualifications

False claims, misleading credentials, impersonation, or fraudulent business representations may result in immediate suspension or permanent removal.

3. Subscription Plans & Billing

Contractor features may require active subscription plans, free trials, monthly billing, upgrades, downgrades, recurring payments, or other billing arrangements processed through third-party payment providers such as Stripe.

Trial periods, bid limits, pricing, features, and subscription terms may change with reasonable notice.

4. Bid Submission Rules

Contractors agree that bids submitted through RevampLEX must be:

  • Private and intended only for the homeowner receiving them
  • Based on honest pricing and realistic scope assumptions
  • Free from deceptive pricing tactics
  • Free from spam, duplicate abuse, or automated manipulation

RevampLEX may limit, monitor, or investigate unusual bid activity, spam submissions, suspicious pricing behavior, or abuse of the platform.

5. One Job = One Bid Opportunity

RevampLEX may count bid usage based on unique homeowner job opportunities rather than the number of estimate revisions or updates made on the same project.

Contractors may revise estimates, proposals, pricing, and scope details on an existing project without necessarily consuming additional bid credits, subject to the active subscription plan.

6. Homeowner Privacy

Contractors acknowledge that exact homeowner addresses, personal phone numbers, emails, and sensitive location details may not be publicly visible by default.

Contractors agree not to attempt to bypass RevampLEX privacy protections, scrape user information, or pressure homeowners into prematurely sharing private contact details.

7. Contractor Privacy

RevampLEX also protects contractor privacy by not necessarily revealing exact contractor addresses or sensitive business information publicly by default.

8. Project Room Conduct

Contractors agree to maintain professional communication in project rooms, including:

  • No harassment, threats, or abusive communication
  • No discriminatory behavior
  • No uploading illegal, harmful, or misleading documents
  • No sharing malware, unsafe links, or malicious files

9. Independent Contractor Relationship

Contractors understand that RevampLEX is a technology platform and not an employer, general contractor, subcontractor, insurer, licensing authority, or party to agreements formed between contractors and homeowners.

10. Reviews, Ratings & Reputation

Contractor ratings, reviews, portfolio content, response times, project completion history, and other performance metrics may be displayed or used to improve homeowner decision-making.

11. Suspension & Removal

RevampLEX may suspend, restrict, investigate, or permanently terminate contractor accounts involved in fraud, chargeback abuse, false licensing claims, spam bidding, harassment, non-payment, or activities that may harm users or the platform.
