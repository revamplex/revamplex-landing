Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026 • RevampLEX is built around privacy, safety, and giving homeowners and contractors control over what they share.

Privacy-first location protection RevampLEX does not publicly reveal exact homeowner addresses, contractor addresses, personal phone numbers, or sensitive contact details during public browsing. Map pins display approximate locations only.

1. Information We Collect

RevampLEX may collect information provided during registration, project creation, account use, messaging, and platform interactions.

  • Name or business name
  • Email address
  • User role
  • Project descriptions
  • Photos
  • Documents
  • Approximate location information
  • License and insurance information
  • Messages and Project Room activity
  • Proposal and estimate information

2. Exact Addresses, Contact Information & Project Connections

RevampLEX does not publicly display exact addresses, emails, or phone numbers during initial project browsing.

When a contractor selects: "Accept & Open Project Room" a project connection is established.

Information that may become visible between connected parties includes:

  • Name
  • Business name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Project location details
  • Photos
  • Documents
  • Project communications

Users remain responsible for exercising judgment when sharing information.

3. How We Use Information

  • Create and manage accounts
  • Connect homeowners and contractors
  • Enable project rooms
  • Enable proposals and estimates
  • Enable scheduling tools
  • Provide AI-powered features
  • Improve platform security and support

4. Project Rooms & Collaboration Features

Project Rooms provide private collaboration between connected users.

  • Messaging and chat
  • Video meetings
  • AI-generated estimates
  • AI Take-Off analysis
  • Photo and document sharing
  • Proposals and estimates
  • Scheduling tools
  • Electronic signatures

Private project room content should not be visible to unrelated users.

5. Payment Information

RevampLEX may use third-party payment providers such as Stripe. Full payment card information is not intended to be stored directly by RevampLEX.

6. Sharing of Information

  • Sharing project information between connected users
  • Sharing Project Room content between connected parties
  • Using trusted service providers
  • Responding to legal requirements

7. AI Services & Generated Content

RevampLEX may use AI tools for:

  • Estimate generation
  • AI Take-Off analysis
  • Proposal generation
  • Document analysis
  • Construction workflow assistance

AI outputs are assisting tools only and should not be considered engineering, legal, architectural, financial, or construction advice.

8. User Control

Users are responsible for information they choose to upload, message, or share.

9. Data Security

RevampLEX uses reasonable measures to help protect user information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Cookies, Analytics & Similar Tools

RevampLEX may use cookies, analytics, and usage logs.

11. Children's Privacy

RevampLEX is not intended for individuals under 18.

12. Changes To This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically.
