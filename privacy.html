Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026 • RevampLEX is built around privacy, safety, and giving homeowners and contractors control over what they share.

Privacy-first location protection RevampLEX does not publicly reveal exact homeowner addresses, contractor addresses, personal phone numbers, or sensitive contact details by default. Map pins are designed to show approximate locations or service areas only.

1. Information We Collect

RevampLEX may collect information you provide directly when creating an account, posting a project, submitting a bid, uploading documents, or contacting us.

  • Name or business name
  • Email address
  • Account role, such as homeowner or contractor
  • Project descriptions, photos, documents, and estimates
  • Approximate project location or service area
  • Contractor profile information, license/insurance details, and business details where applicable
  • Messages, proposals, invoices, appointments, and project-room activity

2. Exact Addresses & Personal Contact Details

RevampLEX is designed so exact homeowner addresses and personal contact details are not publicly displayed on map listings or public cards. Homeowners and contractors decide when to share real addresses, phone numbers, emails, and other private information with each other.

Users should only exchange exact location details or personal contact information when they feel comfortable and are closer to a real project agreement.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to operate and improve RevampLEX, including to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Display approximate map results
  • Connect homeowners and contractors
  • Enable private bids, project rooms, chat, proposals, invoices, scheduling, and document sharing
  • Process subscriptions and billing through third-party payment providers
  • Improve platform safety, support, and user experience
  • Respond to support requests and business inquiries

4. Private Project Rooms

Project rooms are designed to keep communication, bids, proposals, documents, photos, invoices, approvals, scheduling, and messages private between the homeowner and the invited or participating contractor.

Other contractors should not be able to view private bids or files exchanged inside a separate homeowner-contractor project room.

5. Payment Information

RevampLEX may use third-party payment processors, such as Stripe, to manage contractor subscriptions, billing, upgrades, downgrades, and payment-related services. RevampLEX does not intend to store full credit card numbers on its own servers.

6. Sharing of Information

RevampLEX may share information only as needed to operate the platform, provide services, comply with law, prevent fraud, or protect users. This may include:

  • Sharing project details between a homeowner and participating contractors
  • Sharing private room content between the homeowner and the assigned or invited contractor
  • Using trusted service providers for hosting, storage, authentication, email delivery, analytics, and payments
  • Responding to legal requests or safety concerns where required

7. User Control

Users are responsible for the information they choose to upload, post, message, or share with other users. Do not share exact addresses, phone numbers, sensitive documents, or personal details until you are comfortable doing so.

8. Data Security

RevampLEX uses reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect user information. However, no internet platform can guarantee absolute security. Users should use strong passwords, keep login credentials private, and be cautious when sharing sensitive information.

9. Cookies, Analytics & Similar Tools

RevampLEX may use cookies, analytics, logs, or similar technologies to understand usage, improve performance, maintain security, and improve the user experience.

10. Children’s Privacy

RevampLEX is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

11. Changes to This Policy

RevampLEX may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date when appropriate.

12. Contact
