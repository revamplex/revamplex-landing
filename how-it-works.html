The RevampLEX Connection Flow

RevampLEX keeps public browsing private. Exact contact information is not shown during map discovery. A direct project connection starts when a contractor accepts and opens the Project Room.

1

Homeowner Posts a Project

The homeowner adds project details, approximate location, budget, photos, documents, and job notes.

2

Contractors Discover Jobs

Contractors browse nearby opportunities by trade, location, budget, and project type.

3

Accept & Open Project Room

When a contractor accepts, a direct project connection is created and may count as a project connection/bid usage.

4

Collaborate Privately

The connected homeowner and contractor can use chat, files, video, AI tools, estimates, proposals, scheduling, and approvals.

For Homeowners

Post your project, stay protected during public browsing, and work privately with connected contractors.

Post Your Project

1. Describe your renovation or repair project
2. Upload photos, videos, plans, and measurements
3. Add budget, trade type, and approximate project location
4. Keep your exact address and contact details private during public discovery

Use the Project Room

5. A contractor creates a connection by selecting "Accept & Open Project Room"
6. Once connected, identity/contact information may become visible between both parties
7. Share files, ask questions, schedule appointments, review proposals, and approve when ready
Only connected parties should see that private Project Room's chat, files, estimates, and collaboration details.

For Contractors

Find local projects, open a direct workspace connection, and build professional estimates and proposals faster.

Find Local Jobs

1. Browse homeowner projects near you
2. Filter by trade, project type, location, and budget
3. Review visible job details before accepting
4. Select "Accept & Open Project Room" when you want to connect

Build and Manage the Job

5. Opening a Project Room may consume one project connection/bid allocation
6. Use AI Take-Off, 3D Estimate Lab, price book items, and proposal tools
7. Chat, video call, schedule, upload documents, and create customer-ready proposals
AI-generated estimates and take-offs are helpful tools, but contractors must review all quantities, pricing, scope, and assumptions before sending a proposal.

Privacy During Discovery

RevampLEX is designed to protect users during browsing while still making it practical to move forward once both sides are connected.

Before Connection

✓ Approximate locations only
✓ No public exact address display
✓ No public phone or email display by default

After Connection

✓ Project Room becomes active
✓ Identity/contact details may become visible
✓ Both parties can collaborate directly on the project
